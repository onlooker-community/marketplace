# Writing Rubric

Evaluate the submitted writing on the following criteria.

## Criteria

**1. Clarity (30%)**
Is the writing easy to understand on first read? Are sentences clear and
direct? Is jargon avoided or explained?

**2. Accuracy (25%)**
Are all factual claims correct? Is the tone appropriate for the intended
audience?

**3. Completeness (20%)**
Does the output cover all the points requested in the task? Are there
gaps or missing sections?

**4. Structure (15%)**
Is the writing well-organized? Do ideas flow logically? Are transitions
used effectively?

**5. Tone (10%)**
Is the tone consistent and appropriate for the stated audience and purpose?

## Scoring

0.9–1.0: Publish-ready
0.8–0.89: Minor edits needed
0.6–0.79: Needs revision on structure or clarity
0.4–0.59: Requires significant rework
0.0–0.39: Does not meet the task requirements

Point to specific sentences or paragraphs for every weakness.
